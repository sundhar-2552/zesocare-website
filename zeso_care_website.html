About Us

ZESO CARE stands for Zero Stress Orientation or Zen Emotional Support Organization. We are a counseling organization dedicated to providing stigma-free mental health support. We blend traditional counseling with modern digital support to ensure emotional well-being for individuals, families, and communities.

Our Services

  • Individual Counseling (Stress, Anxiety, Depression)
  • Family & Relationship Therapy
  • Child & Adolescent Counseling
  • Digital Detox Programs
  • Mental Health Workshops

Vision & Mission

Vision Statement

To create a stigma-free mental health environment where emotional well-being is nurtured with compassion, evidence-based care, and cultural sensitivity.

Mission Statement

ZESO CARE aims to provide accessible and empathetic psychological services, combining traditional counseling methods with modern digital support for individuals, families, and communities.

Unique Selling Proposition (USP)

  • Affordable, stigma-free care for South Indian communities.
  • Culturally sensitive therapy (Tamil/English support).
  • Digital mental health literacy programs.
  • Peer Counseling + Professional Hybrid Model.
